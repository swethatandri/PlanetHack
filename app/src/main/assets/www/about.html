About LifeBalance

LifeBalance is an app designed for you to record and maintain your health! You can create you own journal, that will record your symptoms, your mood, water intake, & stress level for each day. LifeBalance also allows you to set reminders to follow regular sleeping & eating routines. This schedule feature also allows you to enter in events where you will be leaving home, additionally reminding you to wash your hands after the event.

A balanced life is a crucial part for you to be healthy and well, and LifeBalance is here to help you get balanced!
