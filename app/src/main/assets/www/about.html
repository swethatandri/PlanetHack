About LifeBalance

LifeBalance is an app designed for you to record and maintain your health! You can create you own journal, that will record your symptoms, your mood, water intake, & stress level for each day. In light of this pandemic, LifeBalance also recommends a range of time increments for washing hands as well as provides feedback for your handwashing schedule.

A balanced life is a crucial part for you to be healthy and well, and LifeBalance is here to help you get balanced!
